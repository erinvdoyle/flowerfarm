Our Story

Hi. Welcome to our flower farm. We believe in the joy found in small, everyday moments. Our mission is to offer a retreat from the fast-paced world and a chance to embrace a slower, more intentional way of living.

What's in a Name?

As a couple who left behind the hustle and bustle of corporate America, we've sought a life that harmonizes with nature and brings peace to our hearts. Our vibrant, vital land, full of blooms and blossoms, nurtured through eco-friendly practices, is our sanctuary. But it's not just ours. It also belongs to Elvis, Priscilla, and Marilyn, the first of our flock, orphaned black sheep lovingly raised by bottle. Together, we've embraced our role as the black sheep of the neighborhood, different yet devoted to creating something beautiful and meaningful.

More than a Business

Our farm is more than a business; it's a place to come together and celebrate love and life's special occasions. We offer a picturesque venue for weddings and events, where guests can revel in natural beauty. Each event is infused with the essence of our farm: Sustainability, elegance, and a touch of rustic charm. We also invite guests to experience the tranquility of our farm through garden yoga and meditation classes, a perfect opportunity to reconnect with their inner selves. Our flower arranging workshops and pick-your-own flower visits foster creativity and allow visitors to bring home a piece of the farm.

Walk with Us

By walking through our garden, guests can witness the beauty of sustainable farming and feel the peace that comes from being in tune with nature. Our flower farm stands as a testament to the simple joys that come from cultivating the land, caring for animals, and celebrating the unique path we each walk. We are dedicated to building a community that values sustainability, cherishes small moments of joy, and finds comfort in being wonderfully different.
